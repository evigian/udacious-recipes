A Udacity Study Group

We formed a study group called #sg_real_world_ai_proj in order to study the concepts of this scholarship challenge content while exploring ways of deploying a real world project based in Artificial Intelligence.

After having several online meet-ups discussing about different concepts and concerns about our course content, with the motivation and encouragement of Oudarjya Sen Sarma, we ended up to create a project putting in action this idea initially formed by Aisha Javed.
